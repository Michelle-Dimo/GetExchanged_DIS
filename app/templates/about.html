About GetExchanged

GetExchanged is a web application, designed for the Databases and Information Systems course at the University of Copenhagen.

It is built with Flask, a Python web framework. It is part of a project to qualify for the course exam.

It provides a platform for students to explore and manage exchange agreements with partner universities.

The idea behind the web app was to make a more user friendly and managable platform for students to access and share information about exchange opportunities.

On GetExchanged, you can search for exchange agreements, read reports from previous exchange students, and share your own experiences by writing reports.

On the homepage, you can also find a map of all the partner universities across the world, to easily explore where you can go on exchange.

GetExchanged is meant as a project for a course, and not as a official platform. Use the offical UCPH exchange platform for accurate and up-to-date information.
